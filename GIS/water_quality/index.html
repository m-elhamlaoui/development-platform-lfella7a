About This Analysis

This water quality assessment uses Sentinel-2 satellite imagery to analyze the San Francisco Bay area. The analysis identifies water bodies using the Normalized Difference Water Index (NDWI) and detects potential algal blooms using a chlorophyll index based on red and red-edge bands.

The visualization shows four key aspects:

  1. True Color Image: Shows the area as it would appear to the human eye
  2. NDWI (Water Detection): Highlights water bodies in blue
  3. Chlorophyll Index: Shows potential algal concentrations in water
  4. Water Quality Visualization: Color-coded representation of water quality

Note: This is a Level 1 basic implementation. The results should be validated with in-situ measurements for accurate water quality assessment.
